Sachin Tendulkar

200 Not Out Living Legend

I feel when somebody has been playing cricket for a long time, he creates a separate identity for himself.

Here's a achievement of Master Blaster:

  1. 1994: Arjuna Award recipient for achievements in cricket.
  2. 1997: Tendulkar was one of the five cricketers selected as Wisden Cricketer of the Year.
  3. 1997/98: India's highest sporting honour – Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna.
  4. 1999: Padma Shri – India's fourth highest civilian award.
  5. 2008: Padma Vibhushan – India's second highest civilian award.
  6. 2010: ICC Cricketer of the year – Highest award in the ICC listings.
  7. 2010: LG People's Choice Award.
  8. 2014: Bharat Ratna – India's highest civilian honour.
  9. ICC World Test XI: 2009, 2010, 2011
  10. ICC World ODI XI: 2004, 2007, 2010
  11. Wisden Leading Cricketer in the World 1997, 2010

Beating Pakistan is always special because they are a tough team and we have a bit if a history regarding Pakistan.
